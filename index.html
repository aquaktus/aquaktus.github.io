Oct 2019 - now: PhD at the University of Glasgow

I am developing new machine learning methods to deepen human-machine collaboration by breaking down structured tasks like programming into a conversation. My adviser is Jeff Dalton at the Glasgow Representation and Information Learning Lab. My fundamental motivation is to build agents capable of deep collaboration to achieve complex goals through conversation to enhance rather than substitute humans.
